About Us

Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women formerly known as IGIT, is the first all women engineering University in South Asia. It has been ranked first among the emerging engineering colleges of India.

IGDTUW was established as an independent university in the May 2013, with the purpose of bringing forward women in the feilds of technology and management, and fostering an environment for excellence.

The objective of the university is to foster industry relevant and innovations, and help to enable women to become future leaders, managers, researchers, and team players in the feilds of science, technology, and management.
